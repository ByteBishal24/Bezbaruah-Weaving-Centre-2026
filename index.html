Our Story

Heritage in Every Thread

Nestled in the heart of Guwahati, Bezbaruah Weaving Center has preserved the timeless art of Assamese handloom weaving for generations. Our master weavers breathe life into every thread — carrying forward traditions that have adorned royalty and celebrated culture.

We work exclusively with indigenous fibers: the golden Muga silk, the lustrous Pat silk, the warm Eri — materials that are uniquely Assamese. Every piece is a bridge between the past and the present, crafted on traditional handlooms with patience and love.
